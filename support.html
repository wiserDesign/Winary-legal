Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a wine to my cellar?

Tap the "+" button on the Home screen. You can enter the wine name, vintage, region, rating, and personal notes. You can also add a photo by tapping the image area.

Is my data synced across devices?

Yes. Your wine list is stored securely in the cloud and synced to all devices where you're logged in with the same account.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove your account and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

Can I use Calice offline?

Yes. Calice caches your wine list locally so you can browse and view your cellar without an internet connection. Adding or editing wines will sync when you're back online.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.

How do I report a bug?

Please email us at wisely.corporation@gmail.com with a description of the issue and, if possible, the steps to reproduce it. Screenshots are very helpful!
