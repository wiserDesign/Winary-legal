Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Calice ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Calice:

  • Account data: email address and password (stored securely via Supabase authentication).
  • Wine data: wine names, notes, ratings, and photos you add to your personal cellar.
  • Usage data: app interactions collected anonymously to improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Calice service.
  • To sync your wine list across devices.
  • To send important service notifications (e.g., account-related emails).
  • To improve app performance and user experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure with industry-standard encryption. Photos are stored in Supabase Storage. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Calice uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase – backend, authentication, and storage (privacy policy).
  • Apple / Google – app distribution platforms.

5. Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app's Profile screen. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.

6. Children's Privacy

Calice is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via the app or email.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at wisely.corporation@gmail.com.
